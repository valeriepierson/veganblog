Vegan Tofu Scramble

A classic vegan breakfast option

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 10 min.

Cook time: 15 min.

Total time: 25 min.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package extra-firm tofu
  • 1/3 yellow onion, diced
  • 1/2 pepper, any color, diced
  • 10-15 quartered cherry tomatoes
  • 1 large handful fresh baby spinach
  • shredded vegan chedder
  • turmeric powder
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • garlic & onion powder for seasoning
  • olive oil

---

Directions:

  1. Press tofu until excess liquid has drained, apx. 10 minutes.
  2. Once drained, chop into bite-size cubes and place in lightly oiled pan. Cook over medium heat. Allow tofu to sit in pan and evenly brown, resisting urge to stir.
  3. Once tofu has browned on two sides, season with termeric until all tofu is yellow in color. Season with garlic and onion powder.
  4. Add chopped vegetables -not spinach- and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Once all veggies are cooked, add spinach. When wilted, add chedder and allow to melt.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
